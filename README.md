# Kin-Keeper 🤝

Kin-Keeper is a professional friendship management application designed to help you maintain and nurture your most important relationships. In a busy world, Kin-Keeper ensures you stay connected with the people who matter most through smart tracking and interaction logging.

## ✨ Features

* **Relationship Dashboard:** A clean, high-level view of all your connections with visual status indicators (Overdue, Almost Due, On-Track).
* **Seamless Check-Ins:** Quickly log calls, messages, or video chats to keep your relationship history up to date.
* **Data Visualization:** Interactive analytics powered by Recharts to see how you're spending time with your circle (Call vs. Text vs. Meeting).
* **Modern UI/UX:** Built with a premium "Ocean Deep" theme, featuring a blend of Sora and Manrope typography for a sophisticated feel.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router)
* **Library:** React.js
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **Icons:** Lucide React
* **Charts:** Recharts
